International Conference on Sustainable goals in Materials, Energy and Environment (Kottayam, India, April 25 - 26, 2024) Jan 25,2024

The International Conference on Sustainable Goals in Materials, Energy, and Environment (ICSMEE) is a transferring medium of academic experts and industrialists. Department of Civil Engineering, Mangalam College of Engineering, Ettumanoor, Kerala hosts international conferences every year for the enrichment of knowledge and the development of universal contact for the faculties and students with the outside world.

ICSMEE 2024 will focus on achieving sustainable goals in the means of reproduction, reutilization and reduction of resources used in the field of engineering and technology. For attaining the sustainable goals, ICSMEE 24 forms 4 theme tracks including the upcoming developments of sustainable interdisciplinary approaches in Civil Engineering.



Theme 1: Reutilization of materials and new advanced materials



Theme 2: Reduction of Energy Utilization



Theme 3: Reproduction of Eco-friendly Environment



Theme 4: Advanced Interdisciplinary Approaches



CSMEE 2024 invites full-length original research contributions from science, and engineering professionals from industries, R&D organizations, academic institutions, government departments, and research scholars from across the world.



Deadline for Paper Submission:15th March, 2024



Intimation of Acceptance: 20th March 2024



Camera Ready Paper Submission: 5 th April 2024



Deadline for Registration: 15th April 2024



Releasing of presentation schedule : 20th April 2024


Conference Date : 25th and 26th April, 2024
